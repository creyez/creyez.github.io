About

Hello! I'm Christian Reyes, a current first generation student at Vanderbilt University studying computer science. I am a self-motivated individual. I have been actively seeking ways to develop my skills in order to have a successful future since my participation in NJ SEEDS, a leadership development program that offers high-achieving students from low-income households access to a private school education.

I am also passionate about narrowing the achievement gap, as many of my experiences were educational programs that are for historically excluded communities. One of these programs was All-Star Code, a summer program that focuses on creating economic opportunity for young men of color, as well as the program that helped me find my passion for computer programming. In addition, I attended Google's Computer Science Summer Institute, where I completed a project-based JavaScript processing curriculum taught by Google engineers.

At Vanderbilt, I am currently part of the Questbridge Chapter, FirstVU, the Association of Latin American Students, and the Nu Rho Chapter of Kappa Alpha Psi. I am currently looking for a software developer internship opportunity. I'd be happy to talk about my experiences and skills. Please reach out to me if you have any questions.
